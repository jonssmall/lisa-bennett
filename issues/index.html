Our Platform for Toms River

Building stronger, more resilient communities across Ward 1.

Property Tax Relief

We are all feeling the squeeze of rising costs and local property taxes are no help to our budgets. We need to keep costs down, I will use my decades of experience in Finance to keep prices in check and work for tax relief.

  • Keep costs down
  • Secure tax relief
  • Curb spending

Storm Recovery & Coastal Resilience

New Jersey's coastline is our greatest asset and our most vulnerable point. We need comprehensive storm recovery programs and forward-thinking resilience infrastructure.

  • Enhanced flood protection systems
  • Streamlined disaster relief funding
  • Climate-adaptive building codes

Transparency in Local Government

Now more than ever we need transparency in all levels of government especially at the local level. We need to make our meetings more accessible, not less.

  • Move the council meeting back to 7pm
  • Be responsive to OPRA requests
  • Abide by the Sun Shine Law

Check Overdevelopment

My time on the Zoning Board of Adjustment has shown that I take seriously the problems of Overdevelopment. We need to keep our small town feel and I will continue this fight on the Council.

  • Stick to the Master Plan
  • Be judicious in variance allocation
  • Stop Four-Story and up buildings
