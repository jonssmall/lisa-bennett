Our Platform for Ocean County

Building stronger, more resilient communities across Legislative District 9.

Communication and Transparency in Government

  • Enhance access to essential services for seniors, families, and small businesses.
  • Reform application processes to simplify procedures and eliminate unnecessary bureaucracy.
  • Strengthen communication between government and constituents, ensuring greater transparency and accountability.

Protecting Our Environment and Building a Resilient Future

  • Support legislation that safeguards our waterways from the impacts of coastal storms.
  • Combat pollution by implementing smart controls on overdevelopment.
  • Alleviate traffic congestion through strategic investments in mass transit expansion.
  • Increase funding for sustainable infrastructure, including roads, bridges, and tunnels, to build a more resilient future.

School Funding Reform

  • Back legislation that holds school districts accountable for responsible and transparent spending.
  • Review and modernize outdated and inequitable school funding formulas.
  • Advocate for stable, long-term funding so districts can plan effectively for the future.
  • Require districts to assess infrastructure needs and allocate resources to ensure all students have access to supportive, high-quality learning environments.

Fair Utility Rates & Real Accountability

  • Improve transparency in how utilities operate and spend ratepayer money.
  • Lower costs for ratepayers by capping excessive utility profits
  • Protect investment in infrastructure that strengthens and modernizes the electric grid
  • Advocate for consumer-first reforms, including more frequent independent audits, tighter oversight of utility companies, and enforceable standards for what utilities can charge
