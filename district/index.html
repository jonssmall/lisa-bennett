About Legislative District 9

Legislative District 9 encompasses diverse communities throughout Ocean County, from coastal resort towns to inland suburban townships. Our district represents a unique blend of year-round residents, seasonal communities, and growing suburban areas.

Affordability Crisis

Seniors struggle to stay in their homes, and young families find it difficult to buy their first home. Property taxes, utilities, and healthcare costs continue to rise faster than wages.

Infrastructure & Environment

From coastal flooding to aging infrastructure, our district faces unique challenges that require both immediate attention and long-term planning for sustainable growth.

Quality Education

Supporting our teachers, improving school facilities, and ensuring every child has access to quality education regardless of their zip code or family income.

Healthcare Access

Expanding access to affordable healthcare, supporting mental health services, and protecting reproductive rights for all residents of District 9.
